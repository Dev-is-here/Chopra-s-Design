About

CHOPRA'S DESIGN TRANSFORMS PLACES INTO EXPERIENCES

Chopra’s is the Market Leader in designing, constructing and delivering world-class solutions for acoustics, audio, lighting, projection and stage automation experiences.

Whether it’s creating awe-inspiring spectaculars, complex nightclubs, banquets, auditoriums, residences, theme parks, corporate offices, Chopra’s delivers world-class solutions for such experiences. With its lighting and projection solutions, entertainment automation platform, acoustics (Soundproofing and Sound insulation), stage automation and creative engineering, Chopra’s cutting-edge offerings continue to advance industry standards and exceed client expectations.

Our diverse group of clients include international chains like Playboy, Indian army, government sector, premium residences.

Chopra’s Foresight + Research + Innovation

Chopra’s Designs internal think-tank consists of Foresight, research and continuous innovation. The design and consultancy which deals with the future of the built environment and society at large. We serve Chopra’s global business as well as external clients from a broad range of regions and sectors. We help our organisations understand trends, explore new ideas, and radically rethink the future of their businesses.

About ESI Design

ESI DESIGN TRANSFORMS PLACES INTO EXPERIENCES

NBBJ's New York experience design studio, ESI Design, seamlessly weaves the physical and digital worlds together to create immersive experiences with enduring impact.

From its roots reinventing the Brooklyn Children’s Museum into one of the country’s first interactive museums, the NBBJ studio has defined the field of experience design for over forty years, fundamentally changing how people connect with brands, organizations, cultural institutions and, most importantly, each other.

Its multi-disciplinary team creates solutions that are permanent, yet flexible with designs that grow and evolve to meet the changing needs of both our clients and their audiences.

NBBJ acquired ESI Design in 2020. Today, NBBJ provides unified services - under the direction of licensed architects - that integrate architecture, interiors and experience design resulting in more impactful and streamlined designs.
